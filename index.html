What is Ribbon Health?

Ribbon Health is a powerful digital tool designed to help organizations manage health-related information with ease and efficiency. Unlike typical hospital-based EMS systems, Ribbon Health is crafted specifically for non-hospital organizations, such as non-profits and research institutions, that provide out-of-hospital care and related services.

Key Features

Individual Health Profiles

Create and manage comprehensive health profiles for individuals, including personal information, vitals, administered drugs, and vaccination statuses.

Community Profiles

Set up and manage profiles for groups, enabling efficient healthcare administration for communities.

Inventory Management

Keep track of all medical supplies, consumables, and drugs with an easy-to-use inventory system.

Team Collaboration

Invite team members to collaborate and manage health records together, ensuring everyone stays updated and informed.
